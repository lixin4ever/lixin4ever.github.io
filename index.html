About me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Text Mining Group, The Chinese University of Hong Kong(CUHK). My advisor is Prof. Lam Wai. Prior to that, I obtained the B.Eng degree from School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University in 2016, where I worked closely with Dr. Rao Yanghui and began my research career of natural language processing.
